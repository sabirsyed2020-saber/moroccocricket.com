Let's Grow Cricket Together 🇲🇦🏏

Morocco Cricket is an independent initiative dedicated to promoting and developing cricket throughout Morocco. Whether you are a player, coach, student, volunteer, journalist, sponsor or simply someone who loves cricket, I would be delighted to hear from you.

This website has one simple mission: to help cricket reach more people across Morocco and build a stronger cricket community for the future.
