Why Morocco Cricket Was Created

Cricket has been a passion of mine for many years. Over time, I have been fortunate enough to travel to different parts of the world to experience the sport at its highest level. Watching international cricket in different countries showed me how the game has the unique ability to bring people together, regardless of nationality, language or background.

In 2025, I had the opportunity to visit Morocco. I was inspired by the country's rich sporting culture, welcoming people and the enthusiasm of its younger generation. During my visit, one thought kept coming back to me:

"What if cricket could find a home here too?"

That simple question became the inspiration behind Morocco Cricket.

This website is an independent, non-commercial initiative dedicated to promoting cricket across Morocco. Its purpose is to share news, celebrate the game's history, encourage participation and connect people who believe cricket has the potential to grow throughout the country.

Whether you are a player, coach, teacher, student, volunteer, journalist or simply curious about cricket, you are warmly welcome to be part of this journey.
