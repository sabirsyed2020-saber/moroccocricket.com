🇲🇦 Cricket's Journey Begins

Cricket has a surprisingly rich history in Morocco. Although football remains the country's most popular sport, cricket has been played in Morocco for more than a century and has welcomed touring teams from around the world.

🏟️ Tangier Cricket Ground

One of the most fascinating chapters of Moroccan cricket is the historic Tangier Cricket Ground, once considered among the most unique cricket venues in the world.
