🏏 Morocco Cricket Officially Launches

Published: July 2026

Every great sporting journey begins with a single step.

Today marks the launch of Morocco Cricket, an independent initiative dedicated to promoting and developing the game of cricket across the Kingdom of Morocco.

Our vision is simple: to create a welcoming platform where players, clubs, schools, coaches, supporters and anyone curious about cricket can come together to discover, learn and celebrate one of the world's most popular sports.

Cricket is played and followed by millions of people across the globe. It teaches teamwork, discipline, respect and resilience while bringing together communities from different cultures and backgrounds.

Although cricket is still an emerging sport in Morocco, we believe the country has enormous potential. Morocco has a rich sporting tradition, a passionate young population and a growing international community—all the ingredients needed for cricket to flourish.

Through this website, we aim to share original news, explore the history of cricket in Morocco, highlight international tournaments, celebrate local initiatives and encourage more people to experience the game.

Whether you are an experienced cricketer, someone discovering the sport for the first time, or simply interested in supporting its growth, you are welcome here.

This is only the beginning of an exciting journey.

Together, we can help cricket grow across Morocco.
